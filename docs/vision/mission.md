# Mission

## For Daniel and David

You are reading something your dad built for you.

The world will tell you to study hard so you can *get a good job* — so someone else will
pay you for your time. That's fine. But there is a bigger game, and you are going to learn
it young: **how to create value that didn't exist before, and how to do it for a purpose
greater than yourself.**

That's what a **wealth creator** is. Not someone who is handed money. Someone who **makes
something people genuinely need, does it honestly, and stewards what comes back well.**

## What we believe

1. **Value comes from serving people.** Money is just the world saying *thank you* for
   solving a real problem. Solve bigger problems for more people → create more value.
2. **You learn it by doing it.** Not from a lecture. By shipping one real thing, watching
   real people decide whether to pay, and learning every part of why they did or didn't.
3. **AI is your teammate.** You are growing up at the exact moment when one person with a
   team of AI agents can do what once took a hundred people. Learn to direct that team. Be
   the **mind and the conscience**; let the agents do the heavy lifting.
4. **Character is the real product.** Courage to start. Honesty when it's costly.
   Craftsmanship when no one's watching. Generosity when you succeed. The ventures are just
   the gym where that character gets strong.
5. **We build to bless.** Everything here — every dollar, every product, every skill — is
   meant to build the Kingdom of God: to serve people, to tell the truth, to lift others,
   and to prepare the way. Wealth is a **tool and a test**, never the point.

## The aim, stated plainly

> Raise Daniel and David to be **builders** — people who can take an idea, an AI team, and a
> few dollars, and turn it into something real that serves people and honors God; who can
> make money well and **give it away well**; and who will spend their lives making the world
> look a little more like the Kingdom they're praying for.

The dollar milestones in [`milestones.md`](milestones.md) are the **scoreboard**, not the
**game**. The game is *who you become while you climb*.

## How a father thinks about this

If you reach the biggest number and lose your honesty, your kindness, or your faith — we
failed. If you build something good, treat people right, stay generous, and walk humbly with
God — we won, no matter the number.

But here's the secret: the character that builds the Kingdom is the *same* character that
builds enduring companies. Trust compounds. Generosity attracts the best people. Honesty
lets you sleep and lets others rely on you. **Doing it the right way is also the way that
works.**

Let's build. 🌱

— Dad
