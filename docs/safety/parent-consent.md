# Parent / Guardian Consent

A child participates here **through** a parent, guardian, or approved educator — not around
them. Consent is the front door, not the fine print.

## The principle

> No child contribution becomes public, and no child takes part in a real-world or AI-supported
> activity, without a responsible adult who knows and approves.

## What a parent/guardian is agreeing to

When an adult enrolls a child (in a [Builder Loop](../builder-loop/) or as a contributor), they
confirm they understand:

- contributions are **public and open-source** (MIT) once reviewed and published;
- the child will be identified only by **first name, alias, or project name** — never full
  identity, location, school, or schedule (see [child-safety](child-safety.md));
- real-world steps (talking to people, serving a customer) and AI steps are **adult-supervised**;
- they can request **removal** of the child's contribution at any time, no questions asked.

## How it works (lightweight, on purpose)

1. The adult submits the child's contribution **on the child's behalf** (PR, issue, or the
   form a maintainer points them to). For older children who can use the tools, the adult still
   reviews and approves before submission.
2. The adult states the child's **alias** and confirms consent in the submission.
3. A maintainer keeps the consent record **privately** — it is not published (see
   [privacy-by-design](privacy-by-design.md)).

## Educators and homeschoolers

An approved educator may stand in for the parent's role for children in their care, following
the same rules. A teacher/homeschool version of the Builder Loop is on the
[roadmap](../vision/theory-of-change.md).

## Withdrawing consent

Email a maintainer (see [`SECURITY.md`](../../SECURITY.md)). The child's contribution is removed
promptly. Withdrawing consent is always fine and never penalized.
