# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

This project exists to raise children into builders of good things. **Children read this
repository.** That raises our standard, it does not lower it.

We pledge to make participation a welcoming, safe, and encouraging experience for everyone —
regardless of age, experience, nationality, language, ability, background, or belief.

## Our values in practice

- **Be kind first.** Assume good intent. Critique the work, never the person.
- **Be teachable and teach freely.** Nobody starts knowing everything. Help the next person up.
- **Be honest.** No deception, no plagiarism, no cutting legal or ethical corners.
- **Be encouraging.** A first-time contributor's small PR deserves real warmth.
- **Keep it appropriate.** This is a family-and-global space. Keep language, images, and
  topics suitable for a child looking over a parent's shoulder.

## Faith and inclusion

This project is built by a family motivated by Christian faith, and that *why* is stated
openly in our docs. **Everyone is welcome here** — of every faith and none. We ask only
that everyone treat one another with the respect and love we ourselves are aiming to live
out. Contributions are judged on their merit and kindness, never on a contributor's beliefs.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, hate speech, sexual content, personal attacks, doxxing, spam, or any conduct
that would be inappropriate in front of a child or in a respectful workplace.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and may warn or ban anyone who
violates this code. Report concerns privately to **wjlgatech@gmail.com**. Reports are
handled confidentially and in good faith.
