# Founding Families 🧱

The families who helped build this field lab when it was still unproven — recorded here, with
their consent, **by first name or alias** (never full identity — see
[privacy-by-design](docs/safety/privacy-by-design.md)).

This is the public ledger of [**The Halving**](docs/founding-families.md): the founder's reward
halves each epoch while the cohort doubles. Earliest = most honor, because earliest = most risk
and most work. Like an open-source `CONTRIBUTORS` file, but for the families who showed up first.

> **Want to be on this list?** Apply from the
> [landing page](https://wjlgatech.github.io/daniel-and-david/). For parents/guardians/educators
> only; we add accepted families by first name or alias, with consent.
