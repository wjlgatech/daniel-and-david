One gallery, mixed homes 🏡

The card's host field is what lets us mix — we don't have to choose between hosting on HuggingFace or hosting ourselves. We do both:

  • static — runs in your browser, offline, on your device (our default)
  • space — runs on a HuggingFace Space (for apps that need a live backend)
  • proxy — runs behind our own serverless function (keeps an LLM key safe)
  • cli — a builder tool you run on your own machine
  • concept — designed, not built yet (shown honestly, never faked)
Built kind, honest, and on-device. Cards live in apps/web/public/cards/.
