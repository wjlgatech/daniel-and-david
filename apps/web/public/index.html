Founding Families — The Halving 🧱

We open the field lab to a small number of founding families. Like Bitcoin's issuance, the founder's reward halves at each epoch while the cohort doubles. The earliest families take the most risk and do the most work — so they earn the most honor. The schedule is fixed and public, and we really do stop each epoch at its cap.

Why so few? Because "founding" should mean something. Eight Genesis families means everyone knows each other's names — depth, not scale. It's also the most honest lesson we can give our boys about how scarce, fairly-issued value actually works.

What founding families get: co-design the curriculum, a permanent "Genesis" name, direct office hours — and private early access to the studio's own AI workshop (AnyAgent, Super U, DreamMakeTrue): the same tools we build with, so your kid stops just *using* AI and starts building, growing, and creating with it. The Halving + the perks + how we choose →

Selection is by application and mutual fit — based on research, not wealth, credentials, or how impressive you sound. We never ask your income, your degrees, or your child's grades, school, or name.
