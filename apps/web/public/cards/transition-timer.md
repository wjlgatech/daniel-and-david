## How to run
Open the link. A grown-up taps a preset routine (or builds one with picture steps + minutes), then
hands the device over: the child sees a big picture, a shrinking countdown ring, a 1-minute warning,
and what comes next — and chooses when to move on.

## Notes
Built with [Pain2Gain](https://github.com/wjlgatech/daniel-and-david/blob/main/docs/principles/pain2gain.md)
for the #1 daily pain of a six-year-old (transition refusal). Pure behavioral scaffold: predictability
(what's now / what's next) + agency (child-paced "Next") are what prevent most meltdowns. Celebrates
finishing; never punishes. No LLM, no network, nothing stored online.
