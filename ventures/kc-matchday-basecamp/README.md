# Venture #1 — KC Matchday Basecamp 🏕️

> **A legal, venue-partnered fan-utility + local-commerce business** launched around Kansas
> City's global-football summer. This is the boys' first real venture — the place where
> Daniel and David first feel demand, margin, and shipping for real.

**Independent local business. Not affiliated with, sponsored by, or endorsed by FIFA, any
tournament organizer, any team, venue, or event sponsor.**

---

## What it is, in one breath

A mobile **fan-utility desk** (phone charging, rain/heat essentials, a local guide, group
photo reels) powered by a **web checkout** and **local-business partner offers** — run at
*compliant, venue-partnered* locations, never as stadium-adjacent street vending.

## The offer

| Tier | Product | Price |
|---|---|---:|
| Physical | Rain + Phone Rescue Kit | **$29** |
| Physical | Matchday Ready Kit | **$49** |
| Digital | Group Hype Reel (very high margin) | **$99** |
| B2B | Local Venue Growth Pack | **$250–$750** |

Full SKU list, economics, and rationale are in **[SPEC.md](SPEC.md)**.

## Why this is a great *first* venture

- **Real demand, real deadline.** A live event creates urgency you can't fake.
- **Real margins to learn from.** 60–85% on the right SKUs — you *feel* what margin means.
- **Legal and honest by design.** Explicit compliance gates and a "do-not" list teach the
  most important lesson early: *you win by being trustworthy.*
- **AI-native buildable.** The web app has a complete [PRD and a one-shot build
  prompt](SPEC.md#7-codex--copilot-build-prompt) — a kid + an AI teammate can ship it.

## The hard rules (read before touching anything)

🚫 **Never** use FIFA / World Cup / team / stadium / "official" / "Fan Festival" marks or
imagery. 🚫 **No** food, alcohol, tickets, or counterfeit goods. 🚫 **No** operating without
written venue permission + verified local permits/zoning/sales-tax. 🚫 **Never** store card
details or commit secrets. ✅ Brand is generic + a clear independent-business disclaimer.

These aren't suggestions — they're the [go/no-go gates](SPEC.md#22-hard-gonogo-gates) that
keep the venture (and the family's name) clean.
